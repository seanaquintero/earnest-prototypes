FAQ

Common questions

Will checking my rate affect my credit score?
No. We use a soft credit inquiry to show you your personalized rates, which has no impact on your credit score. A hard inquiry only occurs when you formally submit an application.
How quickly can I get the money?
Same-day funding is available for loans approved before 4pm ET on business days. Most borrowers receive funds within 1 business day after final approval.
What credit score do I need to qualify?
We look at more than just your credit score — we consider your full financial picture. Generally, a credit score of 650+ improves your chances of qualifying for our best rates.
Are there any fees?
None. We charge no origination fees, no prepayment penalties, and no application fees. The APR you see is the APR you pay — nothing hidden.
Can I pay off my loan early?
Absolutely, and we won't charge you a penny for it. Pay off your loan early and you'll only pay interest for the time you actually held the loan.
Do I need to show proof of veterinary expenses?
No. Earnest Pet Loans are personal loans deposited directly to your bank account. You choose how to use the funds — no receipts or vet invoices required.
